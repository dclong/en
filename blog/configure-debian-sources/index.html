Configure Debian Apt Source List

Mar 07, 2019

Things on this page are fragmentary and immature notes/thoughts of the author. It is not meant to readers but rather for convenient reference of the author and future improvement.

Debian Apt Sources List Generator

Personally, I prefer stable with backports. Testing is also good but still update might introduce critial bugs sometimes. It is not easy to maitainly a stable and update to date balance. You can certainly use multi-sources, i.e., use stable, testing, sid and experimental at the same time. multi-source is much harder to maintain especially when you want to support multi-archi It is not recommended.
